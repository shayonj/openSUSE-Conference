--- title: Registrace ---

Registrace začíná 1 června!

openSUSE Conference je kompletně zadarmo. Přesto vám doporučujeme se zaregistrovat co nejdříve. Pokud se zaregistrujete slibujeme vám:

  • Dostanete tašku s dárky včetně plyšového chameleona
  • Bude pro vás k dispozici jídlo a pití
  • Bude pro vás místo na přednáškách!

Prosím pozor: Máme pravidla chování a účastí na konferenci potvrzujete, že je znáte a budete dodržovat!

Registration options

While registration is free, there are two options to support our conference and get some extra perks.

    First of all, we have the Supporter Registration option for those who can spend $50 to support us. You'll get a fun, exclusive gift for this - we won't tell you what it is yet!

    Then there is the Professional Registration option, for a cost of $350 or €250. With this ticket you support the openSUSE Conference and we'll give you something in return:

    • The same exclusive gift as the Supporter Registration
    • Access to a networking session/speaker dinner
    • Access to a feedback session with SUSE executive management team

Registration for the openSUSE Conference will open on June 1st.
