Accomodation

  1. Hotel Krystal was selected as the conference hotel. It’s situated near the conference venue, it can accommodate many people, and it’s easy to find. The hotel is only a few bus/tram stops away from the venue. The distance is four kilometers, so it is, in fact, not that close, but it’s still walking distance.

  2. Diplomat Hotel Prague was second hotel for conference. The 4-star Diplomat Hotel Prague is one of the largest hotels in Prague, it offering a good location and the location is very near the conference venue. The distance is 700 meters away from the venue.

Reservation

  • To reserve a room in the Krystal hotel, contact the hotel directly and tell them, that you are coming for LinuxDays. They have rooms reserved for us, and you’ll get discounted prices. Prices are:

    • 650 CZK per night (breakfast included) single room
    • 1000 CZK per night (breakfast included) double room

  • To reserve a room in the Diplomat hotel please see Diplomat Hotel Prague. They have rooms reserved for us with a special price, but the prices only useful for those who registered the conference and booked hotel before Sept. 21st, if you want to get the special price or booking code, please ask Max Lin or Michal Hrusecky.

    • Note: If you would like to book the Diplomat hotel and already got the booking code, please direct mail Diplomat Hotel with the booking code instead of use theirs booking system!

How to get there

Hotel Krystal

If you are traveling by public transport, the closest stop is tram stop Nad Džbánem (tram 8 and 36). You can use the web of the Prague public transport services to find out how to get there.

By train

If you are coming by train to the Prague main station, you can take underground C in the direction of Háje but get off at the Muzeum station, change the lines to underground A and go all the way to the Dejvická station. From there take tram 8 or tram 36 in the direction of Divoká Šárka and exit at tram stop Nad Džbánem.

By bus

If you are coming by bus and you are arriving at the Florenc station, you should take tram number 8 in the direction of Divoká Šárka and get off at tram stop Nad Džbánem.

By plane

If you are coming by plane to the Praha-Ruzyně airport, take bus 119 and get off at the Divoká Šárka stop and change there to tram 8 or tram 36. It’s their final station, so you can’t go wrong. Exit at tram stop Nad Džbánem.

Diplomat Hotel

The Diplomat hotel is next to the venue and the direction are the same with the venue page.
