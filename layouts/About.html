About The openSUSE Conference

The openSUSE Conference brings together a wide variety of Open Source contributors to collaborate on one of the major Linux distribution projects. In four exciting days of workshops and social happenings we bring alive the openSUSE slogan "Have a lot of fun".

This year, the openSUSE conference co-locates with the Czech LinuxDays event, the SUSE Labs and a Gentoo minisummit. The openSUSE Conference takes place from October 20-23 whereby we will overlap with LinuxDays on Saturday and Sunday (20 and 21).

Check the Navigation menu to learn more about the event and how to participate.

Code of Conduct. It is important for the openSUSE Conference Team to create an environment where everyone feels accepted and welcome. We expect all our visitors to behave according to our Code of Conduct and will take decisive action against any inappropriate behavior.
